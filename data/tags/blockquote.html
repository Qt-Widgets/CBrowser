Blockquote Test

We acted incorrectly in arbitrarily changing the Kumquat Festival date. Quoting from the Kumquat Growers' Bylaws:

The date of the Kumquat Festival may only be changed by a two-thirds vote of the General Membership, provided that a 60 percent quorom of the Membership is present.

(Emphasis mine) Since such a quorom was not present, the vote was invalid.
